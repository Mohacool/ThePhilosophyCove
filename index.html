About the Author

I am a freelance journalist completing my masters in Philosophy at the University of Ottawa. I am fascinated by the tradition of story-telling and the need of the human mind to draw meaning from stories. Story-telling is how we pass on knowledge, stories have the ability to transport, to inspire and to enlighten.
